Extra Value Items

Extra Value meals are special items that encourage unique economic experiences within your local community. This complexity is what gives this category the highest worth.

The requirements for each activity are not thoroughly outlined, but rather challenge you to put in unique effort, energy, commitment and integrity into each experience.

In addition to the experience itself, this project also asks you to create a write up and small presentation for your peers on your experience.

The rest of the details are outlined here!

List of Options

• Interview a banker
• Write a city council member
• Write your state representative
• Job shadow a financial advisor
• Start a fundraiser
• Experiencing foreign exchange rates
• Attend a asset management and liability meeting at a local banker
• Fill out and file your own tax return
• Create a unique and innovative AP Macroeconomics lesson for classroom use
