This website was created by me (Jake V). In total this took me around a week to create and was an ambitious idea that I used for my AP Macroeconomics creative project.

What's this Site?

The main idea of this website is to express my teacher's very intuitive extra credit program. This program called MacroDonalds, is a menu of "opportunities " where you can choose a assignment that will determine how many points you will receive.

Firstly we have the Sides & Snacks which are small movie reviews carrying up to a 20 point bonus!

Second we have the Extra Value items which are the largest and most creative subjects totaling a massive 100 point bonus!

Finally, we have the Desserts & Shakes which give a wide array of books that you can choose to read and review for 50 bonus points!
